Ingredients

  • 250ml pea or chana(yellow pea) flour
  • 2tbsp cake flour
  • 1 onion, grated or very finely chopped
  • 1tsp ground jeera (cumin)
  • 1tsp ground dhunia (coriander)
  • 2tsp crushed dried chillis
  • 1tsp salt
  • 3ml borrie (tumeric)
  • 1/2 bunch chopped green dhunia (coriander) leaves
  • 1 small green apple, grated
  • a few spinach leaves, shredded
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 500nl sunflower oil for deep-frying

Instructions

  1. Combine pea flour and cake flour in a large mixing bowl by sifting.
  2. Incorporate the other ingredients, excluding the baking powder and the oil, and combined with water to create a thick batter.
  3. Mix in baking powder right before frying.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  5. Add 1tbsp of the mixture to the hot oil and fry until slightly brown for approximately 5 minutes.
  6. Flip it and brown the other side.
  7. Remove excess oil using a paper towel or colander while hot.
