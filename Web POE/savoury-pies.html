Ingredients

  • 750g flaky pastry
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 500g steak or mutton mince
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 1tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1tsp crushed garlic
  • 1tsp salt
  • 1tsp ground black pepper
  • 2ml grated nutmeg
  • 2ml ground cloves
  • 1tsp crushed dried chillis or chilli powder
  • 100ml sago soaked in 125ml water for 15 minutes
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, grated

Instructions

  1. Wash mince and drain well.
  2. Braise onions in heated oil until golden, 5-10 minutes.
  3. Add mince and cook for about 15 minutes, stirring, over medium heat.
  4. Add spices and cook a further 15 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Drain sago and add to mince mixture.
  6. Cook, stirring, until sago is transparent, about 10 minutes.
  7. Cut out pastry rounds 7,5 cm in diameter or 7,5 cm squares and place about 10ml mince mixture in centre of half of them.
