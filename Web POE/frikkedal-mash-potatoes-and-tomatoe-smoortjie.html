Ingredients

    Mince frikkadel

    • 1kg of lean beef mince
    • 1 grated tomatoe
    • 1 grated onion
    • 3 to 4 slices of bread soaked in water
    • 1 egg
    • chopped parsley
    • salt and pepper

    Mash potatoe

    • 3 or 4 potatoes
    • water
    • salt
    • butter
    • White pepper
    • Nutmeg
    • Milk

    Tomatoe smoortjie

    • 3 to 4 ripe tomatoes
    • 1 chopped onion
    • Cooking oil
    • salt and pepper
    • sugar
    • Tomatoe past
    • Tomatoe sauce

Instructions

    Mince frikkadel

    1. Mix all the ingredients into a bowl.
    2. Form them into small meat balls.
    3. Heat up oil in a pan.
    4. Start putting frikkedals into the frying pan.
    5. Fry unit nice and brown or dark.

    Mash potatoe

    1. Cut 3 or 4 potatoes and rinse it off.
    2. Place potatoes into pot with cold water and a pinch of salt.
    3. Bring it to a boil until the potatoes are nice and soft.
    4. Drain the water.
    5. Mash the potatoes.
    6. Add butter and milk whilst mashing the potatoes.
    7. Add the white pepper.
    8. Grate a little of the Nutmeg over the mash potatoes.

    Tomatoe smoortjie

    1. Add 1 chopped onion with oil to a pan and braise it.
    2. Add the grated tomatoes to the Braised onions.
    3. Add the tomatoe sauce and tomatoe paste.
    4. Add sugar, salt and pepper.
    5. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
