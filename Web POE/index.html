Our Heritage

The Cape Malay community has a rich cultural history that blends flavors, traditions, and stories. From Bo-Kaap streets to kitchens across South Africa, our food tells a story of family, community, and creativity.

Did You Know?

  • Cape Malay cuisine is a fusion of Southeast Asian, Dutch, and African flavors.
  • Traditional dishes often include aromatic spices like turmeric, cinnamon, and cardamom.
  • Buka is a traditional practice where communities gather to break fast during Ramadan.
