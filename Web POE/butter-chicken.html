Ingredients

  • 12 pieces of chicken fillets chopped in cubes
  • cooking oil
  • 250ml bottle of fresh cream
  • 1 tomatoe
  • 1 tomatoe paste
  • 175ml to 250ml of milk
  • sugar
  • salt
  • butter

Spices

  • 1/2tsp Jeera & Dhanya
  • 1/2tsp Kashmiri Masala
  • 1/2tsp Chicken Masala
  • 1/2tsp Al Masala
  • 1/2tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1/2tsp tumeric
  • Ginger and garlic paste

Marinade

  • Amina butter chicken masala
  • 1/2tsp Jeera & Dhanya
  • 1/2tsp Kashmiri Masala
  • 1/2tsp Chicken Masala
  • 1/2tsp Al Masala
  • 1/2tsp Kashmiri chilli powder
  • 1/2tsp tumeric

Instructions

    Marinade

  1. Marinade cubed chicken fillets with Amina butter chicken Masala.
  2. Add all the ingredients mentioned for the marinade (1/2tsp of all the spices and the ginger and garlic paste.)
  3. Let the chicken marinade for 2 hours or overnight in the fridge.

    4. Method

  4. Add chopped onion and braise it.
  5. Add the rest of the spices your brasied onions.
  6. Add 1 grated tomatoe and tomatoe paste.
  7. Add sugar and salt.
  8. Add your milk.
  9. Add you marinaded chicken.
  10. let it cook for 10 to 15 minutes.
  11. Don't close the pot whilst it's cooking.
  12. Add the fresh cream.
  13. Serve with roti.
