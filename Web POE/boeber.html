Ingredients

  • 2litres milk
  • 1cup vermicelli
  • 100ml sweetened condensed milk
  • 50g saltanas
  • 125g butter
  • 1tbsp rose water
  • 10tbsp(150ml) sugar
  • 75ml sago soaked in water for 30 minutes

    • spices

  • 8 cardomom,
  • 3 stick cinnamom

Instructions

  1. In a pot melt the butter.
  2. Then add the vermicelli and stir with a fork until it turns lightly brown.
  3. Add cardomom, stick cinnamon, and saltanas.
  4. Then add the milk and heat until the milk starts to boil.
  5. Add the soaked sago and let it simmer until it becomes transparent, which should take around 15 minutes, stir it occansionally to avoid sticking.
  6. Combine combine condensed milk, rose water, and sugar, and simmer until thoroughly mixed, approximately for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve while hot.
