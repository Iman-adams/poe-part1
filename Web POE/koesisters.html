Ingredients

  • 1/2tsp ground cardomom seeds
  • 4&1/2 cups of cake flour
  • 1cup of soft mashed potatoe
  • 1&1/2tbsp of grated ginger
  • 1tsp of baking powder
  • 1&1/2 cups lukewarm water
  • 2tsp of ground cinnanom
  • 10grams instant yeast
  • 1 egg
  • 3tbsp melted butter
  • 1tsp mix spice
  • 1tsp of salt
  • 3tsp naartjie zest
  • 2tsp of aniseeds

    • Sugar syrup

  • 2cups of sugar
  • 3cups water
  • 5 stick cinnamom

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a large bowl, and mix well.
  2. Knead the dough for 10 minutes until sticky, and do not add more flour.
  3. Cover with a tea towel and let it rise for 90 minutes in a dark warm area.
  4. Then push down the dough using oil on your hands.
  5. Shape the dough into oval shapes, and fry them in a deep pan on medium heat until both side are brown, then remove from pan.
  6. Make the syrup in a large pan using the listed ingredients until they start to bubble.
  7. Dip koesisters into warm syrup and let is soak for 1 minute.
  8. Finally roll it in coconut and serve.
