Ingredients

  • 1kg of lamb pieces
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 tomatoe paste
  • salt & pepper
  • 1litre water
  • 3tbsp sugar

Instructions

  1. Chop the one onion and place it into a pot.
  2. Wash and add the lamb pieces to the pot.
  3. Add 1 and a half cups for water and add a little oil to the pot.
  4. Cook until all the water has evaporated.
  5. Braise the meat until brown.
  6. Add your grated tomatoe, add your tomatoe paste, and sugar, salt and pepper.
  7. Add your potatoes and a little water.
  8. Let it simmer until potatoes is soft.
  9. Serve with Basmati rice.
