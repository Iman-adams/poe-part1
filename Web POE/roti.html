Ingredients

  • 2cups cake flour
  • pinch of salt
  • 1cup boiling water
  • 4tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. Sift the cake flour into a large bowl.
  2. Add the melted butter to the flour.
  3. Pour the cup on boiling water in th bowl.
  4. Combine them all together using a fork until the you form a wet, lumpy dough.
  5. Once it had cooled down, knead it into a soft dough ball.
  6. The dough will be a litte sticky but continue to knead the dough into a soft smooth ball.
  7. If the dough sticks to the counter or you feel like you losing control of the dough, throw a little flour on the counter and knead the dough until you get your soft smooth texture.
  8. Section the large dough ball into 6 dough balls.
  9. Roll out the dough until it is flat and round.
  10. Heat up a nonstick pan using butter on medium heat.
  11. When you put the roti in the pan heat it for 10 to 15 seconds on each side.
