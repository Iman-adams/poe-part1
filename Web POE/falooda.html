Ingredients

  • 500ml milk
  • 1tsp rose syrup
  • 2 scoops vinilla ice-cream
  • 1tsp falooda seeds

Instructions

  1. Soak falooda seeds in water for 10 to 15 minutes
  2. Then combine the milk, rose syrup and ice-cream, and then blend it well until it is a liquid.
  3. Then add the falooda seeds to the mixture you just made.
  4. Stir is well until combined, and refrigerate for an hour.
  5. Serve in a cup and enjoy.
