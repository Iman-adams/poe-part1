Ingredients

  • 16 pieces of chicken (leg & thigh)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1tsp of ginger and garlic paste
  • 3 potatoes
  • 1 tomatoe (optional)
  • salt to taste
  • 250ml water
  • cooking oil

Spices

  • 1tsp Jeera & Dhanya
  • 1tsp Kashmiri Masala
  • 1tsp Chicken or Mutton Masala
  • 1tsp A1 Masala
  • 1tsp Leave Masala
  • 1/2tsp Kashmeri chilli powder
  • 1tsp tumeric

Whole Spice

  • 4 cloves
  • 2 all spice
  • 2 cardamom
  • 1 stick cinnamom

Instructions

  1. Place cooking oil into a pot.
  2. Put whole spices into oil.
  3. Add 1 chopped onion into the cooking oil as well.
  4. Make onions brown and translucent.
  5. Add all you spices and chicken.
  6. Close lid until source develops
  7. Add your potatoes and salt.
  8. At this stage you can add a little water.
  9. Let it cook for 15 to 20 minutes until potatoes
  10. Serve with Basmati rice or roti.
