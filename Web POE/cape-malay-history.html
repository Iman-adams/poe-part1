Cape Malay cuisine is distinct to Cape Town in South Africa. It is a complex culinary tradition that intertwines its deep history with flavors and spices of the dishes. Cape Malay cooking has a rish history, starting in the 1600s when the Dutch came to the Cape, bringing enslaved people from Malaysia, East Africa, and Indonesia .
